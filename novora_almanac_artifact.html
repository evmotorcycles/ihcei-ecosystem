The bet

When machine intelligence is cheap and everywhere, speed stops being scarce. What becomes scarce is trust — and trust is something you can measure.

Novora's whole stack answers verifiable questions and hands the decision back to the person: Is this claim grounded? Is this agent in-bounds? Is the human still growing? Is the record intact? Is this queue about to fail?

Nothing here mutes a token stream. Every layer calibrates and records — it never censors. That single design choice is why the guard is one people don't switch off.

The stack, in plain words

Thirteen parts, one idea

LISM

The rot law

Organizations fail on a slow slide, not a cliff. The early gauge is enforcement latency τ_v — how long a problem sits unfixed.

IHCEI + NERE

The gatekeeper & its kernel

An epistemological gate — about knowledge, not morality. It scores manipulation and uncertainty on-device and stays silent unless there's a real mechanism.

HELM

The on-device guard

Runs the kernel locally, zero network: scam armor, dark-pattern spotting, a capacity meter, and a tamper-proof wallet.

EI

Epistemological Intelligence

Intelligence that knows what it doesn't know. Composes five proven primitives — Audit · Delegate · Develop · Prove · Hazard — release always stays human.

EI LLM

Eight verifier models

Veridian (deep audit) + Apex (on-device edge). A receiver-side attestation layer that checks generators instead of competing with them.

Echo Database

A ledger you can't secretly edit

Append-only, hash-chained, Merkle-provable. Every decision leaves a tamper-evident receipt; editing history is detected and located.

Page Code

OAuth of agency

A stake-bounded permission table: a coding agent may edit src/** but is physically blocked from force-push, secrets, or payments.

Agency Internet

The machine-to-machine fabric

The addressing and permission protocol so autonomous agents transact on your behalf with bounded, revocable authority.

Novora PAGES

Provenance welded into media

Every second of generated audio/video is bound to its source by a hash chain; a splice or deepfake breaks the chain at the exact spot.

Novora suite · SRE

The 9-product app & swarm compass

Screens content on-device at $0 marginal cost; SRE watches τ_v and throttles an AI swarm the instant its fix-queue saturates.

ADG · C_dev

Network-health telemetry

An organization-graph equation (like E=U·D, not physics): how developed and aligned a network is, from pure graph structure.

TQG-CFE · Ψ

How a node "renders" its world

Alignment decides whether a node experiences ease, hardship, or chaos — a measurable interface, not a metaphor.
