What it means

Novora governs understanding

Hinton's point is about a model's inner capacity. Novora's tools sit one layer out and make that capacity safe and accountable: detect the ambiguity, ground the claim, hold irreversible action until the meaning is resolved, keep the human authoring meaning, and prove the correction in a tamper-evident record. That is understanding you can trust and audit — not just witness.
