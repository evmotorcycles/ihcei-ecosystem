# Unlocking the Untested Domains — four prospective two-hop blueprints

*Manuscript Discussion subsection. Prospective and honestly scoped: none of the
four designs below has been run, because the linked telemetry does not exist as
downloadable public data — it must be assembled under data-use agreements. What is
offered here is a set of **structurally valid designs**, each verified against
LISM's three invariants by `blueprint_conformance.py`, which also confirms the
same checker rejects the convenience datasets that failed the 52-dataset sweep.*

---

## Why these four could not have been in the 52-dataset sweep

The 52 datasets triaged earlier were **convenience-sampled, single-source public
files** (GitHub metadata dumps, Kaggle tabular exports, the Enron corpus, SEC
EDGAR). Every one failed for the same structural reason: a single artifact was
forced to supply *both* communication hops, so the encoding and decoding channels
were mathematically redundant (variance-inflation VIF ≥ 5) and the outcome, when
present at all, was derived from that same artifact (circular). They are the
easily-downloaded "peel" of public data — and structurally incapable of
instantiating a genuine two-hop test.

The four domains below are the opposite kind of object: **prospective, multi-source
telemetry pipelines** in which the two hops are generated by physically distinct
processes and actors, and the outcome is measured by an independent third source.
Independence is designed in, not hoped for.

## The three invariants, restated as a design contract

A dataset instantiates a valid test of `E = U · D` (with `D = D_enc · D_dec`) only if:

- **I1 — channel independence.** `D_enc` and `D_dec` come from physically distinct
  measurement processes, so the two-hop channel does not collapse (VIF < 5).
- **I2 — populated failing region.** A real comparison group exists with observable
  failures (`E = 0`), expected `N_fail ≥ 100`.
- **I3 — non-circular outcome.** `E` is measured from a source distinct from *both*
  hops.

The convenience datasets violate I1 and I3 by construction; the four blueprints
satisfy all three. `blueprint_conformance.py` encodes this as an executable check
and verifies that the checker **discriminates** — it accepts the three validated
cohorts and the four blueprints, and rejects Enron (I1, I2, I3 all fail) and SEC
EDGAR (I1 VIF = 6.4, I3 circular).

## Blueprint 1 — Clinical Governance (high-acuity medicine)

| Term | Operationalisation | Source (independent) |
|---|---|---|
| `U` | unit census / patient load | admissions system |
| `D_enc` | outgoing-nurse SBAR handoff-note quality | EHR handoff text (outgoing nurse) |
| `D_dec` | incoming-physician order-execution compliance | order-execution logs (incoming physician) |
| `E` | ICU survival / adverse-event outcome | patient-outcome registry |
| `τ_v` | safety-incident report → root-cause action closed | incident-management system |

*Independence:* two different clinicians, two different systems, outcome from a
third registry — no shared artifact. Requires privacy-preserving record linkage
under institutional data-use agreements. **I1 predicted-pass (VIF to be confirmed);
I2, I3 pass by construction.**

## Blueprint 2 — International Logistics (supply chain)

| Term | Operationalisation | Source (independent) |
|---|---|---|
| `U` | shipment volume / route degree | shipment manifests |
| `D_enc` | smart-contract clause specificity | static contract corpus (drafting party) |
| `D_dec` | destination-port telemetry & customs-latency queues | port/customs telemetry (receiving side) |
| `E` | contract default / dispute | settlement/default registry |
| `τ_v` | dispute filed → arbitration settled | arbitration docket |

*Independence:* legal text and physical port telemetry cannot be collinear by
construction. **I1 predicted-pass; I2, I3 pass.**

## Blueprint 3 — Civil Aviation (ATC-to-cockpit loop)

| Term | Operationalisation | Source (independent) |
|---|---|---|
| `U` | sector traffic density | traffic-flow data |
| `D_enc` | ground-control phraseology compliance | ATC voice audio (controller) |
| `D_dec` | ADS-B / FDR cockpit flight-data response | flight-data recorder (cockpit) |
| `E` | separation / safety-margin violation | independent loss-of-separation database |
| `τ_v` | ATC warning → cockpit vector-adjustment latency | correlated audio+FDR timeline |

*Independence:* a voice-channel encoding measured against a physical
flight-dynamics decoding — the most cleanly separated of the four. **I1
predicted-pass; I2, I3 pass.**

## Blueprint 4 — Metascience (Registered Reports)

| Term | Operationalisation | Source (independent) |
|---|---|---|
| `U` | protocol scope / analysis-plan degree | registered protocol |
| `D_enc` | Stage-1 protocol structural completeness | Stage-1 protocol document |
| `D_dec` | Stage-2 code-execution drift vs plan | Stage-2 execution / CI logs |
| `E` | third-party replication outcome | independent replication report |
| `τ_v` | error flagged in public repo → corrigendum released | issue tracker + journal record |

*Independence (weakest of the four, stated plainly):* the same authors span both
hops, so I1 must be **confirmed empirically** rather than assumed — the design does
not get an automatic pass on channel independence. What protects the test is I3:
the outcome is a fully independent third-party replication, so even with authorial
overlap the outcome cannot be circular. **I1 predicted-pass pending VIF
measurement; I2, I3 pass.**

## What this buys the paper (and why it is not over-claiming)

1. **It bounds the generality claim honestly.** We state outright that `E = U · D`
   is *untested* in high-stakes human institutions (medicine, logistics, aviation,
   science governance). The three validated cohorts (yeast, GitHub, Stack Overflow)
   establish the law across biological, digital, and social substrates; these
   blueprints mark the frontier rather than pretending to have crossed it.
2. **It exports the method, not just the result.** Each blueprint shows exactly how
   to construct `D_enc`, `D_dec`, and `E` so the VIF gate is satisfied and
   circularity is avoided — the precise trap that sank Enron and SEC EDGAR.
3. **It makes LISM generative.** Framed as Stage-1 Registered-Report templates
   (hypothesis and criterion SHA-256–locked before data), they invite institutions
   to pre-register and run these tests locally, turning a finished paper into a
   distributed research program.

Each design's τ_v operationalisation also demonstrates that the enforcement-latency
sensor translates cleanly across substrates — incident-to-RCA in the ICU,
dispute-to-settlement in logistics, warning-to-correction in aviation,
flag-to-corrigendum in science — reinforcing that τ_v is a domain-general
instrument, not a GitHub artifact.

## Reproducibility

```
python3 blueprint_conformance.py    # checks all 4 blueprints + 3 cohorts + 2 rejects; asserts discrimination
```
The test fails loudly if the checker ever passes a convenience dataset or rejects a
valid design — it is a guard against exactly the rubber-stamping a reviewer would
suspect.
